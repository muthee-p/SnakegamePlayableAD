# SnakegamePlayableAD

Playable ad for my playstore snake game called Nyoks game, which you can find here.
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.Prue.Nyoksgame

A retro-inspired Snake game built around the nostalgia of classic brick handhelds, with a uniquely Kenyan sense of humor. Players take on the role of a farmer who grows snakes, collects valuable skins, and trades them through a local broker named Mras. The project combines simple arcade gameplay with a light trading economy, meme-inspired dialogue, and a playful take on rural hustle culture. I handled the gameplay systems, progression loop, trading mechanics, UI, and overall game design from concept to release.
